Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive care for heart and vascular conditions. Our team of experienced cardiologists uses the latest diagnostic tools and treatment approaches to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

Our Cardiology Services Include:

  • Comprehensive cardiac evaluation
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG)
  • Echocardiography
  • Stress testing
  • Holter and event monitoring
  • Cardiac catheterization
  • Coronary angioplasty and stenting
  • Pacemaker implantation and monitoring
  • Heart failure management
  • Cardiac rehabilitation

Our cardiology team works closely with other specialists to provide integrated care for complex cardiac conditions, ensuring that each patient receives personalized treatment tailored to their specific needs.

Neurology

Our Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our neurologists are experts in managing a wide range of neurological conditions.

Our Neurology Services Include:

  • Comprehensive neurological evaluation
  • Electroencephalography (EEG)
  • Electromyography (EMG) and nerve conduction studies
  • Treatment for headaches and migraines
  • Epilepsy management
  • Stroke prevention and treatment
  • Multiple sclerosis care
  • Parkinson's disease management
  • Memory disorder evaluation
  • Sleep disorder diagnosis and treatment

Our neurology team employs a multidisciplinary approach, collaborating with neurosurgeons, physical therapists, and other specialists to provide comprehensive care for complex neurological conditions.

Dental Care

Our Dental Department offers comprehensive dental services for patients of all ages. Our skilled dentists and dental hygienists are committed to providing high-quality care in a comfortable environment.

Our Dental Services Include:

  • Routine dental check-ups and cleanings
  • Digital dental X-rays
  • Fillings and restorations
  • Root canal therapy
  • Crowns and bridges
  • Dental implants
  • Teeth whitening
  • Orthodontic treatments
  • Periodontal (gum) treatments
  • Pediatric dentistry

We emphasize preventive care and patient education to help our patients maintain optimal oral health. Our modern dental facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure efficient and comfortable treatments.
